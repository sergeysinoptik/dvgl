Philanthropy

Responsibility
If you have a healthy body and mind, I believe you have a responsibility to help those around you.

Democracy and civilization are predicated on the idea that those who have more will protect those who have less — that every life deserves respect. This belief is fundamental for me.

Taking Action
Small things — small kindnesses, planting trees, buying goods for a family in need.

There’s room for anyone to help their communities in ways small or large. If you have the ability to help, I believe you should.

A Philosophy For All
Altruism and empathy are central to the human experience.

I make a challenge to my friends and those I meet in the world to engage more with charity, daily good deeds, and ethical, sustainable business practices, for a better world for all of us.
