Business

Entrepreneurism
I believe business is a vehicle for the transformation of people’s lives.

The struggle of the entrepreneur is shared by the artist and the visionary — namely, to manifest a dream. I’ve been building businesses my entire life, developing and accelerating potent ideas, and this is only the beginning.

Creativity
I’ve always been fascinated with inventors.

The people who foresee new ideas, predict needs, and deliver a product that revolutionizes lives. This creative element of business has always been central to my endeavors, from environmental conservation to personal health, from software development to complex logistics.

Innovation
The future is now, and the needs of people - and society at large - are tangible, identifiable, and urgent.

The time is now for bold thinkers and visionary ideas around new purpose in business — exactly the kind of relevant companies I’m developing with like-minded partners.

My projects

Wise Tribe

was founded in 2020

Myself and Roman Tykhovskyy founded Wise Tribe in 2020, a music label based in Solana Beach, CA, to record and distribute talented world and tribal musicians. The Wise Tribe produces music that transcends genres, from a community who share a deep passion for creating a more beautiful, sustainable, regenerative world.

Ghost Drive

A blockchain based, next-generation security technologies company. After experiencing challenges around cybersecurity and optimization, I met a technologist named Roman Tykhovskyy and learned about one of his projects, GhostDrive. I decided to join him because I believe in the technology we’re developing — that it’s an incredibly useful tool not just for me and my company, but for many people and many companies. Privacy, security and stability are fundamental to a company’s success.

DVL Express

dvl express.com

After driving with several different companies and seeing similar problems - sloppy execution, poor work environment, and sometimes even corruption. I believe drivers deserve better - that PEOPLE deserve better — to be treated with respect. I was my first employee, but soon a number of my friends joined, and word spread that there was a trucking company doing things differently. I took it as my mission to build a better type of company, where people are like family. We hope to stand as an example for what a modern logistics company can look like — professional, ethical and leveraging cutting-edge business techniques and technologies.

Fleettec

Project was born in 2010

FleetTec is an innovative, comprehensive solutions platform to seamlessly connect every aspect of company’s logistics. I decided to develop FleetTec after my company had challenges around efficiency. I couldn’t find a single, one-stop solution to manage warehouses, trucks, drivers, accounting, and the array of other concerns for a logistics company, so we decided to build one. We want to help companies be universally more efficient — and save millions in the process. FleetTec facilitates training in our software, as well as broad consulting and analysis to improve and update a companies best practices and bring them into the era of modern technology.

MarkHam Truck Center

markhamtruck.com

MarkHam Truck Center is a cutting-edge repair shop for semi-trucks and industrial hauling vehicles. I started MarkHam to support both my own and others' companies with the highest quality transport truck repairs and servicing.

Auroom Spiritual Wellness Center

A spiritual wellness center in Chicago that I founded this company with Julia Alimova. Through my own struggles with stress and anxiety, I realized how necessary it was to have community spaces for healing and wellness. At Auroom, people can learn mindfulness practices, meet others who want to grow spiritually, and improve their personal health. The center facilitates sound healings, dance, yoga, meditation, classes and ceremonial healing. Spirituality has helped me to live a more complete, full life, and I want to help bring that healing and experience to more people.

Wise Planet

Wise Planet is a company developed with myself, Steve Bitgood and Roman Tykhovskyy. At Wise Planet, we treasure our natural world, our beautiful planet, and want to help reduce carbon impact in meaningful ways. Our company creates consumer labels and products from easily renewable materials such as cornstarch and other plants. Our goal is to supplant plastic with something rapidly biodegradable, easy to manufacture and customize. Our first products will be coffee cups, transport bags and labels for consumer goods.

Wise Studio

Wise Studio is a state-of-the-art music recording studio in Solana Beach, CA, equipped for commercial and private use. Custom designed and built, outfitted with cutting-edge technology, the Wise Studio is the fruition of a musician’s dream.

American Futsal Academy

When I met Andrew Gavrylyshin, the founder of American Futsal Academy, I was inspired by his passion for the work he was doing, his motivation, his energy and his desire to help. We had a great connection, so I offered to join him in his mission to bring futsal and soccer into the lives of more young people. The Academy trains youths to play futsal, helps them with life-skills through coaching, and teaches them about the importance of fairness, sportsmanlike conduct, and athletics.

UniBox Warehouse

uniboxwarehous.com

As my business at DVL Express expanded, other logistical needs grew, and our logical next step was to develop a warehouse and storage facility. Unibox Warehouse is a 50,000 climate-controlled, private storage company serving the Chicagoland area.
